About Us

Hi I am Kshitija Sawant, a certified International nail CIDESCO certified artist with a passion for creativity and beauty. I have been working in this field for the past few years and love designing unique nail art that makes people feel confident and happy. I specialize in gel nails , nail extensions, and custom nail art like floral, glitter, bridal, and trendy designs. Every design I create is made with care, detail, and high-quality products to ensure it lasts long and keeps your nails healthy. I believe that nail art is not just fashion—it's a way to express your personality. Whether it’s a special occasion or a regular day, I’m here to give your nails a fresh and beautiful look.
